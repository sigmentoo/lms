About Us

At NeoFOCUS-UK (Neonatal Functional Haemodynamics and Point of Care Ultrasound)

we are a dedicated group of UK-based neonatologists, specialising in the application of functional echocardiography and point of care ultrasound (POCUS) in neonatal care.

Our mission is to enhance diagnostic precision and support pathophysiology-based neonatal management in term and preterm infants. With growing evidence supporting the benefits of POCUS, we aim to overcome barriers to training and governance in the UK neonatology community

Why NeoFocus-UK

International surveys consistently reveal the desire among neonatologists to incorporate POCUS into their clinical practice. In the UK, our recent survey indicated a significant interest in learning these valuable skills.

While POCUS and echocardiography training programs are well-established for adult and paediatric critical care, there are no accepted standards for training, accreditation, or application of these techniques in neonatal settings in the UK
