Our Activities!!

Since early 2022, NeoFOCUS-UK has been actively involved in several key activities to promote high-quality training and delivery of neonatal POCUS and integrated haemodynamic approaches in the UK

-CPD & Peer Review: We host weekly online educational meetings attended by neonatal trainees, consultants, and international collaborators. These sessions include expert presentations, case discussions, and challenging cases where POCUS was helpful to guide bedside management

Link for weekly Friday meeting via ZOOM:

- Online Introductory Webinars: These webinars provide an excellent introduction to lung ultrasound.

-National Neonatal Lung Ultrasound, Echocardiography, and Integrated Haemodynamics Conference and workshop. Last one was held in Glasgow in May 2023.

-Workshop on Lung USS in partnership with the local Trusts.

-Frameworks for Practice and guidelines:

We have published frameworks for neonatal lung ultrasound in peer-reviewed journals.
